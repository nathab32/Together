# Together

## About

Together is a device for maintaining connections with friends and companions. Together is heavily inspired by Tamagotchi devices, but differs in that it is collaborative. Born from a graduating senior in need of grad gift ideas, Together hopes to allow friends to stay connected even after they are physically separated.

## Planned Features

- Record voice notes for friends or personal use
- Playback voice notes with integrated speaker
- Start streaks with your friends
- Addressible RGBs to see status of friends/do whatever you want
- Complete tasks with your companions to grow pets!
- Use as microphone for computer/phone or bluetooth speaker
- Tuner
- Metronome
